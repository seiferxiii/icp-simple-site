Empowering Philippine Web3 Communities One Region At A Time

The Challenges

Web3 events in the National Capital Region (NCR) of the Philippines often attract the same faces, limiting diversification and growth. This narrow focus also excludes those in rural areas, preventing them from accessing valuable opportunities.

Our Approach

Layer Zero PH, while focused nationally, founded Web3 Bulacan, to create a local Web3 environment. Supported by governmental frameworks, Web3 Bulacan empowers individuals and startups in Bulacan, aligning with Layer Zero PH's broader mission to expand Web3 across the Philippines.
